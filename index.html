About Us

Dream Queen Spa is a premier wellness destination in Pune, dedicated to providing exceptional spa experiences that rejuvenate both body and mind. Established with a vision to create a sanctuary of relaxation, we combine traditional techniques with modern therapies to deliver unparalleled service.

Our team of certified therapists are trained in various massage modalities and are committed to tailoring each treatment to your specific needs. We use only the finest products and maintain the highest standards of hygiene to ensure your complete satisfaction and well-being.
